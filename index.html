ABOUT ME

Hi,

I'm Arvind, I prefer to call myself "The Raving Sage" on the web but that hasn't actually caught on with other people just yet. The moniker is sort of a twisted anagram of my name and it's something that I put a lot of thought into 😅.

I completed my undergrad in CSE from College of Engineering Trivandrum, graduated in 2019. My interests are scattered all over the place but most of them lie close to the hardware, operating systems, computer networks and computer architecture just to name a few. I currently work as a Systems Engineer in TCS.

I tend to write code once in a while, most of it is updated on Github. I'm not what you'd call a "Gifted programmer", but I always try to challenge myself and get better. Over the years I've sort of fallen in love with the art that I devote lots of my free time learning stuff or writing code. I'm familiar with C, C++, python, bash, assembly, javascript and java in exactly that order.

Like someone once said "Where words stop music begins", I've always been fascinated by music and it's power to elicit emotions and unite people.It's a deeply personal and collaborative experience at the same time. I tend to restrict my singing to the bathroom or in concerts where it is too loud for anyone to hear me but luckily I'm quite comfortable with instruments like the keyboard, guitar and most recently the murali and they allow me to express myself musically to a great extent. These skills that I acquired over many years I completely owe and dedicate to numerous loved ones, my parents, brother, instructors and many others.

A log about this blog

The original intention for this website is to serve as my blog, I've always wanted one. I don't care if nobody actually reads it which probably will be the case, but if you're reading this, now that you reached till here just go on and have a look, stop reading this and read a post, no really go, stop reading, go, go I say😜.

I consider this sort of my diary/immortality project. I assume github is not gonna take down my repo until they run out of namespace, shutdown the company or the armageddon each less likely than the last, so I guess this is gonna stay here for a while. The idea has been in mind since the start of the Corona lockdown of 2020 but I never really got to doing anything. Skip ahead months in and I finally lifted my sorry ass and got to work. I don't plan to stick to any specific topic or even screen my posts for quality, just consider what you see here as rants of mine and nothing more. I guess tagging posts would be a good idea I'll try to get something of that sort set up.

The main aim is to set this site up with the tool statim, which is a tool I'm working on and will hopefully complete. It is intended to be a lightweight static site generator with minimum dependencies, using only bash scripts and tools already available in the linux ecosystem. The idea is not originally my own but of a young colleague of a friend of mine who goes by the name NerdyPeppers. It seemed to be a good idea. The site design as such took next to no work the wonderful and ever helpful people at w3-schools had a free template, I just tweaked it a bit here and there and the results are much better than I expected.
